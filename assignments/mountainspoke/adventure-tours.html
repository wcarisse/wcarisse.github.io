Guided Adventure Tours

Grotto Falls Trail - 2.4 miles

Beginner's Trail: This is a great ride whether it's your first mountain biking experience or you'd just like to take it easy today. The gorgeous waterfalls midway through will inspire you to complete the ride.

  1. Cost: $10
  2. Schedule:
  3. Early Bird: Weekdays at 5:30am
  4. Late Bird: Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 10am
  5. Afternoon: Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday at 1pm

Emerald Lake Trail - 10 miles

Intermediate Trail: Our most popular tour! Enjoy the deep forest and beautiful clear lake as we challenge ourselves to improve our trail riding.

  1. Cost: $18
  2. Schedule:
  3. Early Bird: Weekdays at 5am
  4. Late Bird: Daily at 9:30am
  5. Afternoon: Daily at 2pm

Chesnut Mountain Trail - 15 miles

Advanced Trail: This is the ultimate challenger trail for those mountain bikers that can't get enough of the easy stuff. Increase your stamina and willpower with this guided tour through Chesnut Mountain.

  1. Cost: $20
  2. Schedule:
  3. Early Bird: Weekends at 5am
  4. Late Bird: Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 9:30am
  5. Afternoon: Weekends at 3pm
